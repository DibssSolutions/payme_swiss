Luxury
Luxury payment devices
We are the only program manager worldwide that can create unique and bespoke jewellery payment wearables. Specially the HNWI and UHNWI clients enjoy to have a unique payment product which is tailor-made according to the customer’s ideas. Be it a unique jewellery card, a specially designed key fob with diamonds or a money clip with a 2000 year old coin – we can create all of that.

Turnkey solutions

Our goal is to provide all of the services needed to operate your program, starting from consulting to app development, card and wearable certification and production, and concierge services.

Tokenization – Apple Pay, Samsung Pay, Android pay enabled

Our philosophy is that the end-users shall be able to pay with anything they want to pay with. That is why we decided to enable our cards to be used also at Apple Pay, Samsung Pay and Android Pay

Regulatory compliance

With our experience with management of affluent, HNWI and UHNWI customers and team of leading compliance consultants, we are ready to provide you continuous monitoring of your card program.

Customer onboarding

For each new card program it is important to onboard customers fast and in compliance with the existing laws. We are working in close partnership with leading eKYC providers in order to make onboarding efficient, reliable and fast.

Fraud mitigation

We are using award winning fraud mitigation software to achieve highest security standards for your program.

About us
Payme Swiss AG is a company with a solid track in the card and financial industry.
